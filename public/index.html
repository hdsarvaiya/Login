Mentioned below are the Terms and Conditions that eligible candidates who wish to appear for the on-campus job interviews handled by the Training and Placement Cell of FTE – MSU Baroda (mentioned as 'T&P Cell' henceforth) have to abide by: ('Placements' mentioned henceforth refers to the on-campus as well as off-campus recruitment drives of companies coming at FTE – MSU handled by the T&P Cell or by self.)

  1. A candidate can register for placements through T&P Cell if he/she is a final year student of any academic program of FTE-MSU.
  2. Candidates will have to submit registration details via a form provided by T&P Cell. The details provided should be correct to the best knowledge of the candidate and the same would be verified by the T&P Cell. Your data will be kept confidential and will not be shared with any external entity except the companies coming for placements.
  3. Every company arriving for recruitment via the T&P Cell will have their own eligibility criteria for candidates to appear for their first round of based on (but not limited to) their academic performance in 10th/12th/H Diploma or Degree. Only the eligible candidates according to the said company’s criteria will be allowed to appear for that company’s recruitment drive.
  4. If an eligible candidate appears for the first round of placements for a company, he/she is bound to appear for all the subsequent placement rounds of the said company unless he/she is not eligible for the successive round according to the said company. Failing to do so will result in a penalty as decided by T&P Cell.
  5. Once the candidate is successfully placed via the on-campus placements handled by the T&P Cell or via the off-campus placements by his/her self, then that individual is not eligible to appear for further placements in any company, even if they have cleared any stages in another company conducting placements simultaneously.
  6. A candidate can apply for more than one company and appear in successive rounds simultaneously as long as they are not placed in any company.
  7. If a Candidate has filled the form to appear in a particular company and Is Not shown up for the round without a valid reason , He/She will be blacklisted from the all upcoming placement Drives
  8. If a student secure a job opportunity independently, without assistance from the T&P Cell, it is compulsory for them to promptly provide their placement information to the T&P Cell.
  9. Candidates must have proper decorum during the placement drive including but not limited to attire, behavior, etc. Any indecent behavior or direct contact with company HR/recruiter without concerning department coordinator will lead into penalty as decided by T&P Cell.
  10. The placement rules are equally applicable to the internship drive as well.
  11. If a student participates in a prestigious national or international competitive exam and subsequently secures a job, they have the opportunity to request the withdrawal of their registration from the T&P Cell.
  12. In the event that a timeframe during a student receives results from multiple companies within a 24-hour simultaneous placement drive, they are required to choose one company and inform the respective coordinator within 24 hours. Failure to do so will result in the T&P Cell making the decision on behalf of the student regarding the selection.
  13. Any candidate who gets placed through the T&P Cell will not be granted a Letter of Recommendation for any purpose by the college for duration of one year.
  14. In case of any discrepancies/problems, the final decision lies with the Dean of FTE-MSU and the Professor In-Charge of the T&P Cell.
